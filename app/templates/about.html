Our Mission and Vision

Our Ideal Users
  1. College Students who love volunteering and have ad hoc availability and busy schedules. They can volunteer when they have time (hence might need a calendar feature/aka can add to the user's calendar). However, they prefer variable volunteering (these college students are not free to volunteer every week at a specific time, but free at different times, sometimes on ad hoc basis each week). These volunteers are interested in contributing to their smaller micro-community (eg. frat, sorority, dorms, apartment) or their larger local community.
  2. Older adults who need help with their side projects or volunteering gigs, cannot or do not want to pay to get these volunteering done, and like interacting with and meeting new people through volunteering (aka will volunteer alongside or organize these events)

Why Volunteer?
  1. Meet new people and develop lasting relationships.
  2. Increase your happiness and improve your mental health.
  3. Learn new skills and increase your brain function and self-esteem.
  4. Stand out on job applications and further your career.
  5. Decrease your stress and accrue the health benefits.
  6. Do good in the world and help make a difference.
